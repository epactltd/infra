## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Each Template:

**Template 1 (Initial Notification)**: 
- Within 2 hours of confirmed customer-affecting incident
- P1/P2 severity incidents
- Service outages or significant degradation

**Template 2 (Resolution)**:
- Immediately upon service restoration
- After verification and testing complete
- For all customer-notified incidents

**Template 3 (Data Breach - Individuals)**:
- GDPR Article 34 notification (high risk to individuals)
- After DPO assessment and Managing Director approval
- Within "without undue delay" timeframe
- Clear, plain language; actionable recommendations

**Template 4 (Data Breach - Controller)**:
- When EPACT is processor and customer is controller
- Immediately upon breach discovery (processor duty to notify controller)
- Enables customer to meet their 72-hour ICO notification deadline

**Template 5 (Scheduled Maintenance)**:
- 7 days advance notice (preferred)
- Minimum 48 hours notice (emergency maintenance)
- For any customer-affecting changes

**Template 6 (All-Clear)**:
- 24-48 hours after resolution
- After monitoring period confirms stability
- Reassurance communication

### Customization Required:

**Before sending**, customize:
- [Bracketed placeholders] with actual information
- Specific times (UTC + local timezone)
- Affected services (be specific)
- Contact information (phone number when available)
- Customer-specific impact (if varies by customer)

### Approval Required:

| **Template** | **Approval Needed** | **Timeline** |
|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Template 1 (Initial) | ISMS Lead | Within 2 hours of incident |
| Template 2 (Resolution) | ISMS Lead | Immediately upon resolution |
| Template 3 (Breach - Individuals) | Managing Director + DPO | After GDPR assessment |
| Template 4 (Breach - Controller) | DPO (immediate) | Immediately upon breach discovery |
| Template 5 (Maintenance) | Senior Developer | 7 days before maintenance |
| Template 6 (All-Clear) | Support Team or ISMS Lead | After monitoring period |

### Communication Channels:

**Primary**: Email (to registered customer contact email)  
**Secondary**: Status page update (if available)  
**Tertiary**: In-app notification banner  
**High-Priority**: Phone call (for P1 affecting specific customers)

### Record Keeping:

**Document for each notification**:
- Date/time sent
- Recipients (number of customers/individuals)
- Template used (with customizations)
- Approval obtained (signature/email)
- Responses received (questions, complaints)
- Follow-up actions taken

**File location**: /policies/customer-communications/[Incident-ID]/
